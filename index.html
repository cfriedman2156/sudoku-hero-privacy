Privacy Policy for Sudoku Hero

Last updated: December 2025

1. Information We Collect

Sudoku Hero collects limited information required to provide core app functionality.

  • Email address (for account creation, login, and password recovery)
  • User ID (for account identification)
  • In-app purchase records (to grant in-game currency and prevent fraud)

We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, audio, advertising identifiers, or sensitive personal information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information is used solely to operate the app, authenticate users, process purchases, and maintain account security. We do not use data for advertising, marketing, or tracking.

3. In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple’s App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. RevenueCat is used to validate purchases and deliver in-game currency.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. Data is shared only with essential service providers required to operate the app, such as backend infrastructure and RevenueCat.

5. Data Retention

Account and purchase data is retained only as long as necessary to provide app functionality or comply with legal obligations.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to or deletion of your data at any time.

7. Children’s Privacy

Sudoku Hero is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
