Account Deletion – Sudoku Hero

Last updated: December 2025

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open Sudoku Hero and sign in to your account.
  2. Tap the Profile icon in the bottom-right corner of the screen.
  3. Tap the yellow Options button in the top-right corner.
  4. Select Account, then choose Delete Account.
  5. Type DELETE in the confirmation field.
  6. Tap Continue to permanently delete your account.

Once confirmed, your account and associated data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.
